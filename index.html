THE MASTER HAND, an EECS351 Project by Kevin Li

The Master Hand ominously controls this robot... But he doesn't have total control! Clicking the canvas pauses/unpauses all movement. Dragging your mouse on the canvas allows you to rotate the base of the robot at will!

Some Useful Keys:

  • Up Arrow = Spin Up / Open Hand
  • Down Arrow = Spin Down / Curl Hand
